Your own practice room — for any song you want to learn.

Drop in a track and Rehearsal Room becomes the patient bandmate who plays it as slowly as you need, as many times as you need. Mute the guitar to play along, loop the bridge until it's in your fingers, or drop the key to match your voice.

No subscriptions, no accounts, no uploads — everything runs on your machine. It's open source, so you can read every line, file an issue, or build the feature you wish it had.

Stem separation

Split any song into separate stem tracks — vocals, drums, bass, and "other" — and control the volume of each one independently. Mute the part you're learning and play along with the rest of the band.

Speed control & looping

Slow a song down — without changing its pitch — and create loops to master the tricky parts. Save as many loops as you like, name them, and recall them whenever you sit down to practice.

Pitch control

Need to raise or lower the pitch of the song without changing the tempo? No problem — shift up or down by up to a full octave to match your instrument or your voice.
